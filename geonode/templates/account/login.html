From ASDC to ANHDC

{% blocktrans %} The Afghanistan Spatial Data Center (ASDC), online from 2015 to 2020, has changed its name and purpose to better meet the needs of the Afghanistan National Disaster Management Authority (ANDMA). {% endblocktrans %}

{% blocktrans %} Under its new identity as the Afghanistan Natural Hazards Data Center (ANHDC) it has been handed over to ANDMA. To access the ANHDC and its disaster risk reduction functionalities please register through the following link: http://anhdc.andma.gov.af {% endblocktrans %}

{% blocktrans %} Some ASDC information related to humanitarian coordination and information management are now available in the iMMAP Afghanistan Data Repository, such as map catalogs. {% endblocktrans %}
